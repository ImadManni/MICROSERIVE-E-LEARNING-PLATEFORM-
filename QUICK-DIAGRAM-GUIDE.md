## üéØ Fastest Way to Generate Diagrams

### Step 1: Choose Your Tool

**Option A: Mermaid (Recommended - Easiest)**
- Website: **https://mermaid.live/**
- Best for: Quick generation, interactive editing
- Formats: PNG, SVG, PDF

**Option B: PlantUML (Most Professional)**
- Website: **https://www.plantuml.com/plantuml/uml/**
- Best for: Detailed diagrams, academic papers
- Formats: PNG, SVG, PDF

### Step 2: Copy & Paste

1. Open the website
2. Open the diagram file (`.mmd` or `.puml`)
3. Copy ALL content
4. Paste into the online editor
5. Diagram generates automatically!

### Step 3: Download

- Right-click on diagram ‚Üí "Save image as..."
- Or use download button
- Save as PNG for documents, SVG for editing
